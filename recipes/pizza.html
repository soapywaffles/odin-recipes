Pizza Recipe

Description

It's everyone's favorite dish: Pizza! Made with a incredible pizza crust, and sweet tomato sauce. It also has the most savory cheese and pepperoni.


Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Form pizza dough into a disc shape
  2. Add on pizza sauce, sprinkling the cheese ontop
  3. Finally, add pepperoni slices and bake the pizza at 400 degrees F for 25 minutes
