Chocolate Cake Recipe

Description

This chocolate cake is delicious, yet simple to make. Every inch of it is covered in a chocolate buttercream.


Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Cream the butter and eggs together
  2. Sift in flour and cocoa powder and stir until batter forms
  3. Fill a cake pan with the batter and bake for 350 degrees F for 20 minutes
